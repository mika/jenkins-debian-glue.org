Contribute

How to contribute

jenkins-debian-glue is available under the MIT open source license.
The author welcomes your feedback, bug reports and patches.
Become a contributor and fork the project at github.

The source of this website is also available as Git repository:
https://github.com/mika/jenkins-debian-glue.org
